vacancies

We are always looking for motivated PhD students, Postdoctoral researchers, interns, and external members to work with me. Funding is available on a competitive basis. For enquiries about the research or positions, please send me an email (shanxin.yuan@qmul.ac.uk).

PhDs

Applicants can self-fund their studies.

In addition, multiple fully funded PhD scholarships are available on a regular basis.

  • China Scholarship Council (CSC) Scholarships​, for Chinese applicants.

  • Joint Doctoral Programme in Interactive and Cognitive Environments, joint PhD programme with Univ. Genoa (primary institution)

  • DeepMind PhD scholarships​, for applicants identified as female and/or are of Black ethnicity.

  • Centre for Doctoral Training (IGGI, AIM, Data-Centric Engineering, NERC)​

  • UK BAME Studentships, for UK-BAME (Black, Asian and Minority Ethnic) applicants

  • ANID (Becas Chile), for Chilean applicants.

  • ANII Becas, for Uruguayan applicants.

  • Becas Pronabec- Presidente de la Republica (PhD), for Peruvian applicants.

  • CONACyT (PhD), for Mexican applicants.

  • Marshall Scholarships, for United States of America applicants.

  • Islamic Development Bank Scholarships, for International applicants of eligible countries

  • Pakistan HEC-QMUL PhD Scholarships, for Pakistani applicants.

  • Turkish Embassy Scholarships (PhD), for Turkish applicants.

  • Guatefuturo Scholarships, for Guatemalian applicants.

  • Colfuturo (PhD) for Colombian applicants.

  • Egyptian Bureau for Cultural & Educational Affairs Scholarships (PhD), for Egyptian applicants.

  • Commonwealth PhD Scholarships for low-income countries and high-income countries for International applicants of eligible countries.

Fore more funding opportunities, you can search for our database: Our Scholarships, and Postgraduate Research Studentships.
